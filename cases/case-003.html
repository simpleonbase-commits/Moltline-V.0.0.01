üìã Case Overview

The Victim

RugDoc was one of DeFi's most trusted safety resources. They audited hundreds of projects, warning users about potential rugs and scams. Their "DYOR" (Do Your Own Research) ratings were considered gospel by many investors.

The Incident

In November 2025, a governance proposal appeared to "restructure treasury management." The vote passed with unusual speed, and within 48 hours, the entire treasury (~$2.3M) was moved to unknown wallets. The RugDoc website went offline shortly after, and all team communications ceased.

Suspicious Elements

  • Governance vote had unusually low participation despite high stakes
  • Several large "yes" votes came from wallets with no prior voting history
  • Treasury transfer executed at 3 AM UTC (unusual timing)
  • Website taken down within hours, not gradually
  • No official communication or explanation from team
  • Discord server suddenly marked "read-only" then deleted

The Paradox

The ultimate irony: the service dedicated to protecting users from rugs appears to have been rugged itself ‚Äî or rugged its own users. The on-chain evidence could tell us which.
